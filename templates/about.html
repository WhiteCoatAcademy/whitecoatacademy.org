Our Philosophy

White Coat Academy comprises a team of senior medical students and resident physicians who are deeply familiar with medical knowledge and education. Our recent experiences in medical school have helped shape our view that there is a disconnect between the training of physicians and the way patients learn about and understand their health.

Our mission is to lead the transition of medical education from a proprietary to “open” process. We believe this process should be scalable and agile -- health literacy should be accessible to everyone in a context-appropriate fashion.

With that goal in mind, we have designed and built White Coat Academy.org, a free video library that opens medical education to patients and their families and friends. Our videos are organized by medical speciality and specific disease, which walk you through the basic biology, “who and when” epidemiology, symptoms, and treatment/outcomes of each condition you or someone you know might be facing.

We hope to clearly and concisely extend medical education to patients in an “open” fashion, providing them with the most important information about diseases or conditions they may be confronted with.
